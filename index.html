Welcome to my personal website. I'm passionate about applying machine learning techniques to solve complex problems in physics. Here, you'll find information about my research, projects, and publications.

I am a Ph.D. candidate at CY Cergy-Paris Université, specializing in machine learning applications in physics. My research focuses on classical spin systems, image generation, classification, and regression. I am proficient in C++, C, and Python, with experience in Java, SQL, Spark, and cluster computing.
